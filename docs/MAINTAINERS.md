## Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for:
- Reviewing and merging pull requests  
- Ensuring code quality and consistency  
- Managing releases and versioning  
- Responding to issues and community queries  
- Overseeing project roadmap and direction  

## Becoming a Maintainer
New maintainers may be added based on:
- Consistent, high-quality contributions  
- Demonstrated understanding of the project architecture  
- Community engagement and reliability  

Appointments are approved by the existing maintainers.

## Removing a Maintainer
A maintainer may be removed if:
- They become inactive for an extended period  
- They request removal  
- They repeatedly violate contribution or conduct guidelines
